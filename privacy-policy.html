Privacy Policy for Idea Jar

Last updated: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Idea Jar ("we," "our," or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Locally stored data
Idea Jar stores the following information directly on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to us or any third party:

  • Ideas you save or favorite
  • Ideas you mark as completed, along with the date
  • Your filter preferences (people, location, cost, duration, energy)
  • Whether you have unlocked Pro features

Purchase information
If you purchase Idea Jar Pro, the transaction is processed entirely through Google Play's billing system. We do not collect, see, or store your payment details (card numbers, billing address, etc.). Google may share limited transaction information with us (such as purchase confirmation and a transaction ID) solely to verify your purchase and unlock Pro features. For details on how Google handles this data, see Google's Privacy Policy.

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Your location data
  • Analytics or usage tracking data
  • Any data that identifies you personally

How We Use Information

Since all app data (saved ideas, completed ideas, preferences) is stored locally on your device, we do not have access to it and cannot use it for any purpose. Purchase confirmation data is used solely to verify your Pro purchase.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share any user data with third parties. We do not have a server that stores your personal app data.

Data Retention and Deletion

All app data is stored locally on your device. You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing the app's storage in your device settings.

Children's Privacy

Idea Jar is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app does not require any account creation or personal information to use.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
